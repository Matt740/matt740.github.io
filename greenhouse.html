Greenhouse Prototype

This was a simple introductory project for my Praxis 3 class. I am very happy with the level of industrial design that my team of 3 accomplished in this design. I worked for the most part on the structural design of the box and the integration of the circuit components. This project included Raspberry Pi with CircuitPython, Fusion 360 modeling, and AutoCAD for the lasercutting.
